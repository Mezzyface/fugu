## What's imported here

A curated graybox subset lives under `game/assets/prototype/` (9 PNGs pulled from
the pack's **Light** set): `grid_fine`, `grid`, `grid_diagonal`, `checker`,
`crosshair`, `wall`, `door`, `window`, `stairs`. These are the documented
fallback for any missing sprite/texture — see `docs/art_direction.md` (#9) and
`game/assets/prototype/README.md`. Assembled example in
`game/scenes/prototype_demo.tscn`.

Imported for issue #25.
